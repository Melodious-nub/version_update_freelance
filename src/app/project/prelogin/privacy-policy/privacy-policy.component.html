Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 February 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DadyIN ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our product management platform and related services (the "Services"). It also explains how we handle information in connection with our integrations with Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram (Meta Platforms) and other third-party services.

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, password, phone number, job title, and company details when you register or update your profile.
  • Business information: Company name, address, industry, and other business data you enter in the Services.
  • Content: Product data, orders, communications, and any other content you create or upload.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: How you use the Services (pages visited, features used, time spent).
  • Device and technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and similar technical identifiers.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies as described in Section 6.

2.3 Information From Third-Party Integrations

When you connect or use our integrations with Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram, we may receive:

  • From Facebook / Meta: Profile information (name, profile picture, email if permitted), pages you manage, ad account information, and data needed to provide the integration (e.g., posting, analytics).
  • From LinkedIn: Profile information (name, headline, profile picture, email if permitted), company page data, and data needed for LinkedIn-related features.
  • From Instagram: Profile information, media, insights, and data needed for Instagram business or creator features (Instagram is part of Meta).

The exact data we receive depends on the permissions you grant when you connect these accounts and the features you use. We use this information only to provide and improve the integrated services you have requested.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you.
  • Process transactions and send related information (e.g., order confirmations).
  • Communicate with you (e.g., support, updates, marketing where permitted).
  • Enable integrations with Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram (e.g., publishing, analytics, advertising).
  • Personalize your experience and present relevant content.
  • Analyze usage and trends to improve our Services and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with vendors who help us operate the Services (e.g., hosting, analytics, email, support). These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.2 Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram (Meta / LinkedIn)

  • When you connect your Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram accounts, we share only the information necessary to provide the integration (e.g., content to post, identifiers to link accounts).
  • Data shared with these platforms is also governed by their respective privacy policies:
    • Facebook Privacy Policy
    • Meta Privacy Policy (covers Instagram)

  • We encourage you to review these policies and your privacy settings on each platform.

4.3 Legal and Safety

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of us, our users, or the public.

4.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy commitments.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated data subject to applicable law and our retention requirements.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in and remember your preferences.
  • Understand how you use our Services (analytics).
  • Support security and fraud prevention.

When you use features that involve Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram, those platforms may also set their own cookies or similar technologies when you interact with their widgets, buttons, or content on our Services. Their use of data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Services or third-party integrations.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format).
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent (e.g., marketing).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights or to disconnect Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram from our Services, use the account or integration settings in the Services or contact us using the details below.
